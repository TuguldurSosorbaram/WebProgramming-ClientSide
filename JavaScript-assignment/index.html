Rules:


  • Goal of the game is to fill all white tiles with light
  • Light bulbs can only be placed above white tiles.
  • The light spread only straight line from light bulbs.
  • Light can't penetrate through black tiles
  • Black tiles with numbers must have exactly same number of light bulbs on the adjecent sides
  • Two light bulbs can NEVER illuminate each other!
  • After you finished you can click on CHECK button to check your solution
